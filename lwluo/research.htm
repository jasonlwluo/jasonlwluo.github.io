Research

Research Interests

My past main interests are gravity, cosmology and particle physics as well as field theory. In particular, I concentrated on the alternative gravity theories with the problems of the late-time acceleration, cosmic magnetic fields and extra dimensions. I am currently a member of the KAmioka GRAvitational wave (KAGRA) experiment and work on the topics related to the gravitational wave (GW) physics.
