Research Interests

My past main interests are gravity, cosmology and particle physics as well as field theory. In particular, I concentrated on the alternative gravity theories with the problems of the late-time acceleration, cosmic magnetic fields and extra dimensions. I was a member of the KAmioka GRAvitational wave (KAGRA) experiment and studied the gravitational-wave (GW) physics. Currently I am working on the gravitational physics, quantum anomalies and the physical problems with the applications on machine learning/deep learning (ML/DL).
